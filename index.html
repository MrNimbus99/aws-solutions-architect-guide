What is the Solutions Architect Associate Certification?

The AWS Solutions Architect Associate (SAA-C03) certification demonstrates your expertise in designing cost-optimized, secure, and scalable AWS architectures. It's ideal for solution architects, cloud engineers, and anyone designing AWS infrastructure.

Exam Details: 65 questions | 130 minutes | $150 USD | Passing score: 720/1000

Key Exam Domains

1. Design Secure Architectures (30%)

  • IAM policies, roles, and permissions
  • VPC security (Security Groups, NACLs)
  • Encryption at rest and in transit
  • AWS security services (GuardDuty, Inspector, Macie)

Practice these concepts with NestedCerts' 1,200+ SAA-C03 security questions.

2. Design Resilient Architectures (26%)

  • Multi-AZ and multi-region deployments
  • Auto Scaling and load balancing
  • Disaster recovery strategies
  • Decoupling with SQS, SNS, EventBridge

3. Design High-Performing Architectures (24%)

  • Compute services (EC2, Lambda, ECS, EKS)
  • Storage solutions (S3, EBS, EFS, FSx)
  • Database selection (RDS, DynamoDB, Aurora)
  • Caching strategies (CloudFront, ElastiCache)

4. Design Cost-Optimized Architectures (20%)

  • EC2 pricing models (On-Demand, Reserved, Spot)
  • S3 storage classes and lifecycle policies
  • Cost monitoring with Cost Explorer and Budgets
  • Right-sizing and resource optimization

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Overcomplicating solutions: AWS prefers simple, managed services over complex custom solutions
  • Ignoring cost optimization: 20% of the exam tests cost-effective design
  • Skipping hands-on practice: Theory alone isn't enough - use the AWS Free Tier
  • Not reviewing wrong answers: Flag difficult questions and review them multiple times
